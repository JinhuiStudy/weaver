Voice · DO / DON'T

개발자 톤. 평서문. 감탄사·이모지 금지. 메트릭으로 말하기.

DO · 쓰자

  • "Deployed to prod · v3 is live"
  • "Eval gate failed — decision_accuracy 87.2% < 90%"
  • "p95 latency 1.84s (−12% vs prev week)"
  • "3 consecutive 503s · retrying in 32s"
  • "Shadow 10% · divergence 3.2% · promote ready"
  • 한국어: "배포 완료 · v3 라이브"
  • 숫자는 항상 mono font · tabular-nums.

DON'T · 쓰지 말자

  • "🎉 Awesome! Your agent is live!"
  • "Something went wrong 😢"
  • "Unleash the power of AI agents"
  • "Oops! Looks like there's an issue."
  • "Supercharge your workflow with Weaver!"
  • 한국어: "에이전트가 멋지게 배포됐어요! 🚀"
  • 가독성 없는 불필요한 이모지 · 감탄사.
